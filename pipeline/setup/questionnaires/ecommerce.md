# Questionnaire — E-commerce / product

For businesses selling physical or digital products to consumers. Ask the shared
core (`_router.md`) first, then these.

## Product

1. What do you **sell** — single hero product, a range, or a category?
2. What makes it **better/different** from what's on the shelf or online?
3. Who **buys** it, and what occasion or need triggers the purchase?
4. Key **benefits and features** (materials, ingredients, specs) that matter to buyers.
5. **Price point** and where it sits (budget, mid, premium)?

## Trust & objections

6. **Reviews/ratings** — average and count? UGC or photos from customers?
7. **Guarantee / returns / warranty** terms?
8. **Shipping**: cost, speed, free-over-threshold, regions served?
9. Any **press, awards, certifications** (organic, cruelty-free, B-Corp, etc.)?
10. Common **hesitations** before buying (fit, quality, delivery)? → FAQ.

## Conversion

11. Primary action — **buy now, shop the range, join waitlist, get a discount**?
12. Selling **on-site** here, or linking to Shopify/Amazon/another store?
13. Any **launch offer / discount / bundle** to feature?
14. Email capture for **list-building** (newsletter, first-order discount)?

## Brand

15. What **feeling** should the product/brand evoke (premium, playful, natural, bold)?
