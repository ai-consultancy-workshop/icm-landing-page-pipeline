# Questionnaire — Agency / studio

For creative, marketing, design, or development studios that do client work and have
a portfolio. Ask the shared core (`_router.md`) first, then these.

## Offering

1. What **services** do you provide? What's your signature / most-wanted offering?
2. Who do you do your **best work for** (industry, size, stage)?
3. What **transformation/result** do clients get?
4. What's your **process** (discovery → … → delivery) in a few steps?

## Proof (portfolio sells)

5. **Selected work / case studies** — which projects best represent you? Visuals/links?
6. **Results/metrics** from past work you can quote?
7. **Client logos** you can show? **Testimonials** with names/roles?
8. **Awards, press, recognition**?

## Conversion

9. Primary action — **book a call, start a project, request a proposal**?
10. Do you publish **pricing / engagement model** (project, retainer, "from £X")?
11. Any **availability / "now booking"** signal to create urgency?

## Identity

12. Your studio's **personality** in a few words (bold, minimal, playful, premium)?
13. Anything visual you're **known for** that should carry into the site?
14. **Team** — feature the people, or keep it work-forward?
