# Questionnaire — SaaS / software

Ask the shared core (see `_router.md`) first, then these. Conversational, not a form.

## Product

1. What does the product **do**, in one sentence a non-expert would understand?
2. What **problem** does it solve, and what do people do today without you?
3. Who is the **ideal customer** (role, company size, industry)? Who is it *not* for?
4. Top **3–6 capabilities/benefits**, each framed as an outcome for the user.
5. How does it **work** in 3–4 steps (sign up → … → value)?

## Proof

6. Any **customers, logos, or press** you can show?
7. **Metrics/results** you can quote (e.g. "saves 5 hours/week", "98% faster")?
8. **Testimonials or case studies** available? Names/roles/companies usable?
9. Integrations, certifications (SOC 2, GDPR), or security points that build trust?

## Conversion

10. **Pricing** — show tiers on the page, or "contact us"? What are the plans?
11. Is there a **free trial / freemium / demo**? Credit card required?
12. What's the **primary CTA** (Start free, Book a demo, Get started)?
13. Any **objections** you hear repeatedly (price, switching cost, security)? → FAQ.

## Differentiation

14. Who are the **alternatives/competitors**, and why do you win?
15. One thing you'd want a visitor to **remember** after leaving?
