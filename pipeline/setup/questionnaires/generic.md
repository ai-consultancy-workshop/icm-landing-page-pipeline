# Questionnaire — Generic

Fallback for any business that doesn't fit a specific set, or to top up a chosen set
with missing detail. Ask the shared core (`_router.md`) first, then these.

## What & why

1. In one sentence: **what do you do and for whom?**
2. What **problem** do you solve, or what **need** do you meet?
3. What makes you **different** from the alternatives?
4. What are your **top 3–6 things** a visitor should know (offerings/benefits)?

## Who & proof

5. Who is your **target visitor**, and what are they trying to achieve?
6. What **proof** can you show (results, testimonials, logos, numbers, credentials)?
7. How does working with you **work** (a few simple steps)?

## Conversion

8. What's the **one action** you most want a visitor to take?
9. **Primary CTA** label, and a secondary if any?
10. What **objections or questions** come up most? → FAQ.

## Brand & content

11. How should the brand **sound** (3–5 adjectives) and **feel** visually?
12. **Assets** available: logo, colours, fonts, photos, existing copy?
13. Anything to **avoid** (words, claims, styles, competitors)?
14. The single thing you'd want a visitor to **remember**?
