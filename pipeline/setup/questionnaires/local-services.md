# Questionnaire — Local services

For businesses serving a local area in person (trades, clinics, salons, restaurants,
gyms, home services). Ask the shared core (`_router.md`) first, then these.

## Service & area

1. What **services** do you offer? Which are most profitable / most in demand?
2. What **area** do you serve (towns, radius, postcodes)?
3. Are you **mobile/on-site**, do customers come to you, or both?
4. **Hours**, and do you offer emergency/out-of-hours service?

## Trust (decisive for local)

5. **Reviews/ratings** (Google, Trustpilot, Checkatrade) — score and count?
6. **Credentials**: licences, insurance, certifications, accreditations, awards.
7. **Years in business**, jobs completed, or other proof of track record.
8. **Photos**: real work, before/after, team, premises, vehicles?
9. **Testimonials** from named local customers?

## Conversion

10. Primary action — **call, book online, request a quote, get directions**?
11. Do you want a **booking/contact form**, a phone number, or both prominent?
12. Typical **pricing** signal — fixed prices, "from £X", free quotes/estimates?
13. Any **guarantees** (satisfaction, workmanship, price match)?

## Local SEO

14. The exact **business name, address, phone** (NAP) as it should appear.
15. **Key services + locations** you most want to be found for.
